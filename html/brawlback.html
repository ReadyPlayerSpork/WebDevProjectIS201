What is brawlback?

Brawlback is an ambitious community-driven project dedicated to integrating rollback netcode into Super Smash Bros. Brawl, significantly enhancing the online gameplay experience.

What is rollback netcode?

Rollback netcode, celebrated for its ability to provide smooth and responsive online matches, ensures that players can enjoy near-lagless battles, even when competing from distant locations. Communities that were previously limited by distance will now be able to experience Brawl with friends as if they were sitting next to you!

This groundbreaking enhancement will also be fully compatible with popular mods like Project +, preserving the unique features and balance these mods bring to the game.
By uniting the passionate Smash Bros. community, Brawlback aims to revolutionize online play, making it more competitive and enjoyable for players around the globe.
Supported Mods:
  • Project +

    • Previous version compatibility will be coming soon
  • Project M 3.6
  • PMEX Remix
  • and many more!
